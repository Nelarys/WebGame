Quick Guide

How to play Mastermind:


Mastermind has two roles, the role of the codebreaker and the role of the codemaker. The codemaker makes a code of colored orbs, while the codebreaker tries to guess said code by proposing his own code of colored orbs. After each guess by the codebreaker, the codemaker sets pins for each correct sphere. For each white pin that is set, there is one sphere with the right color. For each red pin that is set, there is a sphere with the right color and place. The codemaker can use this to adjust their guess every round and (hopefully) crack the code.
